How's it going Y'all?

Allow me to introduce myself...


My name is Miguel Lugo and I'm currently studying computer science at Oregon State University. #gobeavs
I'm also a blue belt in 10th Planet Jiu-Jitsu and have had the chance to train in many gyms all over the country. I first started training Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu back in 2011, but didn't start taking it serious until 2016.
Although I live in Oregon, I got my blue belt through my instructor in 10th Planet Atlanta in May of 2018. I was fortunate enough to find my instructor after losing to a 16 year old that trained as the same school in a local tournament.
My favorite aspect of the 10th Planet system is the focus on jiu-jitsu for MMA and the unorthodox aspect of technique when compared to traditional BJJ schools. I also am a huge fanatic of the fact that the school is primarily no-gi, since it makes you learn how to grapple without relying on the traditional kimono and makes things more realistic for self-defense situations.
