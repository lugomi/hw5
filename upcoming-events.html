Upcoming Combat Sports Events

These are some upcoming events that I'm beyond stoked for. This summer with restrictions getting loosened, us fans are going to be treated to some amazing events in all realms of combat sports. My personal favorite to look out for is Combat Jiu-Jitsu Worlds. Of course, I have also included other events that are worth watching!

  • Professional Grappling Federation Season 2 Finale

    May 21, 2021


    This past season of the PGF has been a very exciting one. We got to see some of the best heavyweights of grappling that the southeast to offer with some up-and-coming guys as well as some established black belts that have competed all over the country.

  • Submission Underground 23

    May 23, 2021

    This has to be one of the best grappling promotions in the country, based out of the PNW and run by West Linn's very own, Chael Sonnen.

  • Combat Jiu-Jitsu Worlds: The Welterweights

    June 6, 2021


    Of all the events listed, this has to be the one that I'm most looking forward to. Some of the best athletes at the 170 weight class will prove to show who is the toughest grappler. The two people to watch for are Nathan Orchard and Roberto Jimenez.

  • UFC 263

    June 12, 2021


    Yay, another Diaz fight! /s
